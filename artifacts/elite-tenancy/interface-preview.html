Frequently Asked Questions — London
How does Elite Tenancy differ from Rightmove in London?
Unlike Rightmove, we include AI tenant matching, full referencing, and legal packs — all for a single flat fee. No monthly subscription and listed until let.
What are the average rents in London in 2026?
Average rents vary by zone: Zone 1–2 from £2,100–£3,500/mo; Zone 3–4 from £1,400–£2,100/mo. Our listings average £2,850/mo across inner London.
Does the Renters Rights Act 2025 apply in London?
Yes. The Renters Rights Act 2025 applies across England including London, abolishing fixed-term tenancies and Section 21 no-fault evictions. Our legal pack is fully updated.
